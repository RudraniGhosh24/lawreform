## Demo Video Script (3 minutes max)

### Opening (0:00–0:20)
- Show the problem: "Over 500 million Indians can't access legal aid"
- Quick montage: daily-wage worker, tenant, rural woman
- "What if they could just speak their problem and hear their rights?"

### Demo — Hindi Voice Scenario (0:20–1:30)
1. Open ai.lawreformer.com on a phone
2. Select Hindi (हिंदी) from language selector
3. Tap the mic button (show pulsing animation)
4. Speak: "मकान मालिक मेरा किराया वापस नहीं कर रहा"
5. Show live transcription appearing on screen
6. Show the transcription — user can edit before submitting
7. Submit — show Gemma 4 streaming the response
8. Highlight the law citation badge: [Rent Control Act, WB 1997]
9. Show the response being read aloud in Hindi
10. Show the Next Steps panel: "Contact Rent Controller, your district"
11. Show footer: "Built by Rudrani Ghosh · lawreformer.com"

### Demo — English Scenario (1:30–2:10)
1. Switch to English
2. Tap scenario tile: "How do I file an RTI?"
3. Show streaming response with RTI Act citation
4. Show next steps: "File at rtionline.gov.in →"
